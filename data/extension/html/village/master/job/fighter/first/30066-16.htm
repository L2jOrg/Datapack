Grand Master Pabris:
Elven Scouts have protected our sacred forest for millennia. Their stealth and cunning allow them to destroy invaders and then silently melt back into the forest.
The most elite Elven Scouts are chosen to be Plains Walkers. Most become Silver Rangers and acquire the ultimate mastery of archery. Talent is not enough, however! Endless practice is necessary to develop their skills.
Those wishing to transfer to the Elven Scout class must pass the test administered by Master Reisa and obtain a recommendation letter from him.
