Warehouse Chief Reed:
To become an Scavenger, you must have reached at least level 20 and have passed the Test of the Scavenger.
There is one more requirement, however. Only those who have experienced the great Human world beyond the Spine Mountains are qualified to change profession. Then and only then will we entrust you with such rarefied skill and knowledge.
First, take the Test of the Scavenger from Collector Pippi of the Bronze Key Guild over there. She will tell you everything you need to know. If you succeed, then travel to the Human village called Gludin and seek out Warehouse Chief Moke. If he deems you ready, he will change your class to Scavenger. Good luck!
